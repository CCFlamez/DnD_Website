An Introduction...

The group known as the Fast and the Furrious (also known as Stultus Gratis, also known as Stultus Cerinitus, also known as the Kaivos, and one that has been redacted for political reasons), have been adventuring for the past year or so. They've helped many individuals, cities and political figures across the continents, and commited various crimes in order to achieve their goals. While most of their plans end up in flames (not metaphorically), they are filled with determination, courage, and stupidity. With these qualities they stand proudly in the face of danger, and will challenge any foe who stands in their way.
